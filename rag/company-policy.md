# HireFlow Company Overview

## About HireFlow

HireFlow is an AI-powered recruitment software company headquartered in **Uttara, Sector 10, Dhaka, Bangladesh**.

The company has approximately **50 employees** working across engineering, product, customer success, operations, sales, and administration.

HireFlow's mission is to simplify recruitment by combining modern software with artificial intelligence, helping organizations hire faster while improving the candidate experience.

---

## Products

HireFlow develops recruitment software including:

- AI Recruitment Platform
- Applicant Tracking System (ATS)
- AI Resume Screening
- Resume Parsing
- Candidate Evaluation
- Candidate Ranking
- Recruitment Analytics
- Hiring Workflow Automation

These products are designed for businesses of all sizes to streamline recruitment and improve hiring efficiency.

---

## Recruitment Philosophy

HireFlow believes hiring decisions should be fair, transparent, and based on qualifications.

Artificial Intelligence is used to assist recruiters by analyzing resumes, matching candidates to job requirements, and generating recommendations. Final hiring decisions are always made by authorized hiring personnel.
